    Ministry Structure

    Each ministry is headed by a Minister who is part of the Council of Ministers and reports to the Deputy Prime Minister. Ministries are responsible for:

    • Developing policy in their area of responsibility
    • Implementing government programs and initiatives
    • Managing government agencies and services
    • Coordinating with international counterparts
    • Serving Georgian citizens and residents
