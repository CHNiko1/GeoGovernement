    Key Governance Principles

    Democratic Accountability

    All government officials are accountable to the people of Georgia and subject to democratic oversight.

    Transparency

    Government decisions and actions are made public to ensure citizens can participate in governance.

    Rule of Law

    All government actions operate within constitutional and legal frameworks that protect citizen rights.

    Efficiency

    Government services are designed to be responsive, effective, and respectful of citizen time.
